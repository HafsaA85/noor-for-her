Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

We care deeply about your privacy and want to be clear about what information we collect and how we use it.

1. What we collect

When you create an account, we collect basic details like your email and password. If you use trackers or notes, we store the information you choose to add so we can show it back to you.

2. How we use it

We use your data to provide and improve the Noor for Her tools, personalise your experience, and keep your account secure. We do not sell your personal data.

3. How we store it

Your data is stored securely and only for as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.

4. Your choices

You can request to update or delete your account by contacting us. If you delete your account, we will remove or anonymise your personal data where possible.
