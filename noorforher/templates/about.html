About Noor for Her

Noor for Her is a safe, faith-aligned space for Muslim women who want to understand their anxiety, notice their triggers, and gently build a healthier, calmer inner world.

Hey, I’m Hafsa

I’m a Mindset & Confidence Coach for women who want emotional peace, clarity, and inner strength. But before I ever became a coach, I was the child and even when became a woman struggling silently with anxiety.

I know what it feels like to wake up with a heavy heart…
To overthink every decision…
To feel like my mind was running faster than I could breathe…
To smile on the outside while feeling overwhelmed on the inside.

There were days when even simple things — a conversation, a message, a task — felt like too much. And I kept thinking:

“Why can’t I just be normal?”

But the truth was…
I wasn’t broken.
I was overwhelmed, unheard, and emotionally tired.
And that realization changed everything.
